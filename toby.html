Puppyhood

Mommy and Daddy adopted me from SPCA in Monroe, CT when I was a wee little 4 month old pupster. I was scared at first but my new big brother, Pom Pom, took lots of care of me and showed me how to be dog!

Hobbies

I don't have the same energy level as other pomeranians so my favourite activities are much more laid back.

In my free time, I enjoy:

  • Spinning in circles when I am excited
  • Strolling around in the dog park
  • Nudging mommy and daddy's hand for pets

About Me

I am a six pound little boy! I have black and tan fur that gets tangled up often (not my fault!) but Mommy tries to keep me photo ready by chasing after me with a brush.

I am quite cheerful and anytime I am about to get a treat, I get so excited that I spin in circles. Sometimes, I even accidentally spin myself out of the room!

Favourite Treats

  • Bell Peppers (they are so crunchy)
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Shrimp
  • Clementines (My favourite)

Pom Pom is my brother and best friend. I follow him everywhere, unless he jumps onto the couch. Unfortunately, I am just too short to get up on furniture without my parents coming to the rescue.

I enjoy stealing on chew sticks that Pom Pom is in the midst of eating. I don't think he minds...too much.
