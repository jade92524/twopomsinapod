Puppyhood

Hello! I was born on October 2nd, 2012. As a Libra doggy, I am clever, imaginative and witty...and feisty? I joined my family in December of 2012, adjusting from rural Pennsylvania to the cosmopolitan city of Stamford, Connecticut. So many more things to poke around and sniff at!

Education

I am a proud graduate of Pet Smart's puppy kindergarten. This is my graduation day where Mommy made me wear a silly hat for a photo op. I am not a fan of dressing up, but I behaved for fear of not receiving my diploma otherwise!
